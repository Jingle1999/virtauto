Most factories have energy data. Few have decision logic.

Energy dashboards show consumption. Production systems show cycle status. But they rarely answer the operational question that matters: Can we reduce load now without risking quality, takt or stability?

Governance problem

§ 5 — Governance

Bounded. Explainable. Safe by default.

virtauto does not optimize blindly. It checks whether optimization is allowed under current operational conditions.

Decision Contract — Admissibility Rules
  • Advisory-only. No autonomous execution.
  • Default HOLD when evidence is missing or stale.
  • Protected stations cannot be delayed.
  • Quality and cycle stability take precedence over cost savings.
  • Every recommendation is limited to one line and one time window.
  • Operator or production lead review is required before action.
