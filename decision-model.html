Decision Model

virtauto introduces a dedicated decision layer that orchestrates, validates and governs actions across agents and enterprise systems.

The Missing Layer

Modern industrial systems generate data, context and AI proposals. But they lack a structured way to decide what is allowed to happen next.

What exists today

ERP defines constraints, MES reflects reality, AI generates proposals. But decisions remain implicit and ungoverned.

What is missing

A system that explicitly models, validates and governs decisions before execution.

Decision Graph as Governing Backbone

virtauto treats decisions as structured objects inside a graph of constraints, authority and outcomes — not as side effects of systems.

Decision Nodes

Explicit decisions with identity and context.

Authority

Defines who is allowed to decide.

Constraints

Define allowed and forbidden paths.

Outcomes

Approved, blocked or escalated actions.
