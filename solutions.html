GEORGE

GEORGE — Generic Entity Orchestrating Resources & Gathering Efficiency

The supervisory brain of virtauto.OS. Plans, routes, validates and learns across all domain agents.

Request demo See responsibilities

Responsibilities

  • Task Orchestration: break down intents, assign to domain agents, monitor progress.
  • Policy & Guardrails: enforce constraints (cost/safety/data access), human-in-the-loop when needed.
  • Memory Management: maintain short-/long-term and entity memory; share scoped context.
  • Tool Governance: register MCP tools/APIs, rate-limit & audit usage.
  • Evidence Tracking: require citations/logs; generate explainable outcomes.
  • KPI Feedback: measure impact (OEE, scrap, lead time) and learn optimal playbooks.

Interfaces

Northbound (L4)

ERP/PLM/SCM via APIs & events (orders, BOMs, changes).

Southbound (L3–L1)

MES, SCADA/OPC UA, CV/IoT streams through gateways.

Tool Registry

MCP catalog of reusable tools (RAG, simulators, optimizers).

Example Flow

“Quality status for Part X, Line 3, Week 34?” → GEORGE routes to Quality + Production + Supply Chain agents → validates evidence (MES, QMS, supplier ASN) → returns answer + next-best-actions.

Security & Compliance

  • Role-based access; tokenization where required.
  • Audit trail of agent/tool calls; prompt & policy red-teaming.
  • Environment separation and rate limits per site.
