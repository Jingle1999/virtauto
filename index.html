What is an AI Agent?

Agents are autonomous systems that reason, plan, use tools, and collaborate to achieve goals—going beyond static LLM prompts. In virtauto.OS, specialized agents take responsibility for quality, sourcing, production, finance, and more—each with clear roles, tools, guardrails, and memory. GEORGE coordinates them.
