virtauto.OS

Industrial decision intelligence for production and energy.

virtauto.OS connects production data, energy signals and operational constraints into a governed decision layer for industrial operations.

The first focus is on energy-intensive production lines: especially Body-in-White processes, SCADA/MES/ERP data and traceable recommendations based on ALLOW / HOLD / BLOCK.

First Use Case

Energy Scan for BIW production lines.

The Energy Scan analyzes production and energy data locally in the browser and shows where a line is losing energy — without data upload, without write access and without intervention in production.
