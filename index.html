What is an AI Agent?

Agents are modular units orchestrated by GEORGE to optimize operations, quality, and supply chain resilience.

1. Basic ResponderLLM answers human-like questions

3. Self-ConfigAgents call APIs, execute actions autonomously
4. Multi-AgentDomain agents cooperate, GEORGE orchestrates
