Why a Decision Layer?

Industrial environments already generate massive amounts of telemetry, machine and process data. However, most systems still optimize locally instead of making coordinated operational decisions.

virtauto.OS introduces a governed decision layer between operational data and execution systems. The platform evaluates risks, constraints, energy costs, quality targets and operational priorities before recommendations are issued.
