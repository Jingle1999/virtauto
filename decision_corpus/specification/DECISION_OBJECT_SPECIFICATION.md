# Decision Object Specification (DOS) v0.1

## Purpose

The Decision Object is the atomic unit of Decision Intelligence.

It represents a single governed decision together with its operational context, governance, evidence and outcome.

Decision Objects are immutable.

Knowledge evolves by creating new Decision Objects and relationships between them.

---

## Design Principles

- Immutable

- Traceable

- Explainable

- Governed

- Reproducible

- Machine-readable

- Human-readable

---

## Core Elements

Every Decision Object shall contain:

- Identity

- Context

- Governance

- Evidence

- Decision

- Outcome

- Relationships
