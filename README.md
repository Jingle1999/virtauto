# virtauto.OS

Industrial Decision Intelligence Platform for governed Multi-Agent Systems.

virtauto.OS is an industrial operating layer that connects operational data, agent-based reasoning, governance contracts and human decision-making.

Core focus areas:

- Industrial Decision Intelligence
- Governed Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)
- Energy Optimization
- Runtime Governance
- Human-in-the-Loop Operations
- Industrial AI Pilots

## Current Capabilities

- Runtime Surface with governed decision flows
- BIW Energy Intelligence showcase
- Decision contracts and audit trails
- Synthetic industrial runtime simulation
- Governance-first agent orchestration
- Energy scan workflows
